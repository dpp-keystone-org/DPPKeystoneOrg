Description

Information regarding the temperature ranges the battery can withstand and time spent in extreme temperatures.

governedBy: DIN DKE SPEC 99100

Properties

Property Description Type governedBy
Temperature range idle state, lower boundary (dppk:temperatureIdleLower) The lower boundary of the surrounding temperature range the battery can withstand when not in use. xsd:double Annex XIII (1l)
Temperature range idle state, upper boundary (dppk:temperatureIdleUpper) The upper boundary of the surrounding temperature range the battery can withstand when not in use. xsd:double Annex XIII (1l)
Time spent in extreme temperatures above boundary (dppk:timeExtremeTempUpper) The aggregated time in which temperatures above the upper boundary of the temperature range are prevalent. xsd:double Annex VII Part B (4)
Time spent in extreme temperatures below boundary (dppk:timeExtremeTempLower) The aggregated time in which temperatures below the lower boundary of the temperature range are prevalent. xsd:double Annex VII Part B (4)
Time spent charging during extreme temperatures above boundary (dppk:timeChargingTempUpper) The aggregated time in which the battery is charged while temperatures above the upper boundary are prevalent. xsd:double Annex VII Part B (4)
Time spent charging during extreme temperatures below boundary (dppk:timeChargingTempLower) The aggregated time in which the battery is charged while temperatures below the lower boundary are prevalent. xsd:double Annex VII Part B (4)
