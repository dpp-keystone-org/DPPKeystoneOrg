Attributes

Any raw, semi-worked, or worked product which is exclusively composed of textile fibres, regardless of the mixing or assembly process employed.

subClassOf: dppk:Product

Properties

Property Description Type
Fibre Composition (dppk:fibreComposition) Relates the textile product to its constituent fibres and their proportions. dppk:FibreComposition
Presence of Non-Textile Parts of Animal Origin (dppk:animalOriginNonTextile) Indicates the presence of materials such as leather, bone, or shell. xsd:boolean
Tear Strength (dppk:tearStrength) dppk:QuantitativeValue
Abrasion Resistance (dppk:abrasionResistance) Resistance to wear, often measured in Martindale cycles. dppk:QuantitativeValue
Microplastic Release Potential (dppk:microplasticReleasePotential) Information regarding the propensity of the textile to release microplastics during washing or use. dppk:QuantitativeValue
Care Instructions (dppk:careInstructions) Standardized care symbols (e.g., GINETEX codes). xsd:string
Repairability Information (dppk:repairabilityInformation) Instructions or scores related to the ease of repairing the textile product. rdf:langString
