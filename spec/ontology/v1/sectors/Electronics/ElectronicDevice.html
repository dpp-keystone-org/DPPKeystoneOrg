Attributes

Equipment which is dependent on electric currents or electromagnetic fields in order to work properly.

subClassOf: dppk:Product

Properties

Property Description Type
Voltage (dppk:voltage) The electric potential difference, typically measured in Volts (V). dppk:QuantitativeValue
Rated Power (Wattage) (dppk:ratedPower) The power consumption of the device under normal operating conditions, typically measured in Watts (W). dppk:QuantitativeValue
Energy Efficiency Class (dppk:energyEfficiencyClass) The energy efficiency rating as defined by the EU Energy Labelling framework (e.g., A, B, C). xsd:string
Ingress Protection (IP) Rating (dppk:ipRating) Defines levels of sealing effectiveness against intrusion from foreign bodies (tools, dirt) and moisture (e.g., IP54). xsd:string
Spare Parts Availability (dppk:sparePartsAvailable) Indicates whether spare parts are available for the product. xsd:boolean
Service Manual Availability (dppk:serviceManualAvailable) Indicates whether a service or repair manual is available. xsd:boolean
Software Update Policy (dppk:softwareUpdatePolicy) Information on the duration and type of software/firmware updates provided by the manufacturer. rdf:langString
WEEE Category (dppk:weeeCategory) The category under the Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment (WEEE) Directive. xsd:string
Disassembly Instructions (dppk:disassemblyInstructions) Instructions for safely disassembling the product for repair or recycling. rdf:langString
