Attributes

A tool that is actuated by an additional power source and mechanism other than the solely manual labor used with hand tools.

subClassOf: dppk:ElectronicDevice

Properties

Property Description Type
Torque (dppk:torque) The rotational force generated by the tool, typically measured in Newton-meters (Nm). dppk:QuantitativeValue
Maximum Rated Speed (dppk:maximumRatedSpeed) The maximum operating speed of the tool, typically measured in Revolutions Per Minute (RPM). dppk:QuantitativeValue
