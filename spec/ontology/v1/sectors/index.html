Class: Battery (dppk:Battery)

A product that supplies electrical energy generated by direct conversion of chemical energy, as defined in Regulation (EU) 2023/1542.

Properties

Property Description Type
Battery Category (dppk:batteryCategory) The category of the battery (e.g., Portable, LMT, EV, Industrial, SLI). xsd:string
Battery Chemistry (dppk:batteryChemistry) The chemical composition of the battery (e.g., Lithium-ion, Nickel-metal hydride, Lead-acid). xsd:string
Date of Manufacture (dppk:dateOfManufacture) xsd:date
Rated Capacity (dppk:ratedCapacity) The total electric charge the battery can deliver under specific conditions. Usually measured in Ampere-hours (Ah) or Watt-hours (Wh). dppk:QuantitativeValue
Nominal Voltage (dppk:nominalVoltage) dppk:QuantitativeValue
Expected Lifetime (dppk:expectedLifetime) The anticipated service life of the battery, often expressed in charge/discharge cycles or years. dppk:QuantitativeValue
State of Health (SoH) (dppk:stateOfHealth) A measure of the battery's condition relative to its ideal new condition. Applicable to EV, LMT, and Industrial batteries. xsd:decimal
Battery Carbon Footprint (dppk:batteryCarbonFootprint) Relates the battery to its declared carbon footprint information. dppk:BatteryCarbonFootprint

Class: Battery Carbon Footprint (BCF) (dppk:BatteryCarbonFootprint)

Information related to the carbon footprint of the battery, calculated according to the relevant Product Environmental Footprint Category Rules (PEFCR).

Properties

Property Description Type
Total Carbon Footprint (dppk:bcfTotal) The total declared carbon footprint of the battery, expressed in kg CO2 equivalent per functional unit. dppk:QuantitativeValue
Carbon Footprint Performance Class (dppk:bcfPerformanceClass) xsd:string
Carbon Footprint Declaration Web Link (dppk:bcfDeclarationWebLink) A web link to a public version of the study supporting the carbon footprint declaration. xsd:anyURI
