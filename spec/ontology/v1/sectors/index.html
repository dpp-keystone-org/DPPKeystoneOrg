  • Battery.jsonld

    DPP Sector Ontology - Battery Definitions (v1)

    Defines sector-specific concepts required for the Battery Passport as per Regulation (EU) 2023/1542.

    Properties

    • dppk:batteryCategory - The category of the battery (e.g., Portable, LMT, EV, Industrial, SLI).
    • dppk:batteryChemistry - The chemical composition of the battery (e.g., Lithium-ion, Nickel-metal hydride, Lead-acid).
    • dppk:dateOfManufacture
    • dppk:ratedCapacity - The total electric charge the battery can deliver under specific conditions. Usually measured in Ampere-hours (Ah) or Watt-hours (Wh).
    • dppk:nominalVoltage
    • dppk:expectedLifetime - The anticipated service life of the battery, often expressed in charge/discharge cycles or years.
    • dppk:stateOfHealth - A measure of the battery's condition relative to its ideal new condition. Applicable to EV, LMT, and Industrial batteries.
    • dppk:batteryCarbonFootprint - Relates the battery to its declared carbon footprint information.
    • dppk:bcfTotal - The total declared carbon footprint of the battery, expressed in kg CO2 equivalent per functional unit.
    • dppk:bcfPerformanceClass
    • dppk:bcfDeclarationWebLink - A web link to a public version of the study supporting the carbon footprint declaration.

  • Construction.jsonld

    DPP Sector Ontology - Construction and Heavy Industry Definitions (v1)

    Defines concepts for Construction Products (aligning with CPR) and related heavy industries such as steel and rail.

    Properties

    • dppk:hasDoP - Relates the construction product to its Declaration of Performance.
    • dppk:dopIdentifier - The unique identification code of the Declaration of Performance.
    • dppk:harmonisedStandardReference - A reference (URI or identifier) to the applicable Harmonised European Standard (hEN) used for assessing the product.
    • dppk:notifiedBodyID - The identification number of the Notified Body involved in the assessment and verification of constancy of performance (e.g., NB1234).
    • dppk:avsSystem - The system for Assessment and Verification of Constancy of Performance (e.g., System 1, 1+, 2+, 3, 4).
    • dppk:reactionToFire - The classification of the product's contribution to fire (Euroclass).
    • dppk:thermalConductivity
    • dppk:compressiveStrength
    • dppk:steelGrade - The classification of the steel based on its composition and physical properties (e.g., R260, S355).
    • dppk:railProfile - The standardized cross-section design of a railway rail (e.g., 46E1).

  • Electronics.jsonld

    DPP Sector Ontology - Electronics and Electrical Equipment Definitions (v1)

    Defines concepts for Electronics and Electrical Equipment (EEE), aligning with regulations such as RoHS, WEEE, and Eco-Design requirements.

    Properties

    • dppk:voltage - The electric potential difference, typically measured in Volts (V).
    • dppk:ratedPower - The power consumption of the device under normal operating conditions, typically measured in Watts (W).
    • dppk:energyEfficiencyClass - The energy efficiency rating as defined by the EU Energy Labelling framework (e.g., A, B, C).
    • dppk:ipRating - Defines levels of sealing effectiveness against intrusion from foreign bodies (tools, dirt) and moisture (e.g., IP54).
    • dppk:sparePartsAvailable - Indicates whether spare parts are available for the product.
    • dppk:serviceManualAvailable - Indicates whether a service or repair manual is available.
    • dppk:softwareUpdatePolicy - Information on the duration and type of software/firmware updates provided by the manufacturer.
    • dppk:weeeCategory - The category under the Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment (WEEE) Directive.
    • dppk:disassemblyInstructions - Instructions for safely disassembling the product for repair or recycling.
    • dppk:torque - The rotational force generated by the tool, typically measured in Newton-meters (Nm).
    • dppk:maximumRatedSpeed - The maximum operating speed of the tool, typically measured in Revolutions Per Minute (RPM).

  • Textile.jsonld

    DPP Sector Ontology - Textile Definitions (v1)

    Defines sector-specific concepts required for the Textile DPP, based on the EU Strategy for Sustainable and Circular Textiles.

    Properties

    • dppk:fibreComposition - Relates the textile product to its constituent fibres and their proportions.
    • dppk:fibreType - The specific type of fibre (e.g., Cotton, Polyester, Wool, Elastane).
    • dppk:fibrePercentage - The percentage of the total product weight constituted by this fibre.
    • dppk:animalOriginNonTextile - Indicates the presence of materials such as leather, bone, or shell.
    • dppk:tearStrength
    • dppk:abrasionResistance - Resistance to wear, often measured in Martindale cycles.
    • dppk:microplasticReleasePotential - Information regarding the propensity of the textile to release microplastics during washing or use.
    • dppk:careInstructions - Standardized care symbols (e.g., GINETEX codes).
    • dppk:repairabilityInformation - Instructions or scores related to the ease of repairing the textile product.
