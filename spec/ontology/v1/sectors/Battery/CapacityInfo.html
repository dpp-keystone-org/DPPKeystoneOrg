Attributes

Information regarding the battery's capacity, energy, state of charge, and voltage limits.

governedBy: Annex XIII (1g, 1h, 4d)

Properties

Property Description Type governedBy
Rated capacity (dppk:ratedCapacity) The total number of ampere-hours (Ah) that can be withdrawn from a fully charged battery under reference conditions. xsd:double Annex XIII (1g); Annex IV Part A (1)
Remaining capacity (dppk:remainingCapacity) The corresponding in-use data attribute to rated capacity, as information on the state of health of the battery. xsd:double Annex VII Part A (1)
Capacity fade (dppk:capacityFade) The decrease over time and upon usage in the amount of charge that a battery can deliver at the rated voltage, with respect to the original rated capacity. xsd:double Annex IV Part A (2)
Certified usable battery energy (dppk:certifiedUsableEnergy) The usable battery energy according to the procedure in the UN GTR No 22 as determined during the certification. xsd:double
Remaining usable battery energy (dppk:remainingUsableEnergy) The usable battery energy at the present point in the lifetime of a battery. xsd:double
State of certified energy (SOCE) (dppk:soce) The measured or on-board usable battery energy performance at a specific point in its lifetime, expressed as a percentage of the certified usable battery energy. xsd:double Annex VII Part A (EV)
State of Charge (SoC) (dppk:stateOfCharge) The available energy or charge in a battery expressed as a percentage of rated capacity. xsd:double Annex XIII (4d); Article 3 1(27)
Minimum voltage (dppk:voltageMinimum) The lower voltage limit that the safe operation of the battery is rated for. xsd:double Annex XIII (1h)
Maximum voltage (dppk:voltageMaximum) The upper voltage limit that the safe operation of the battery is rated for. xsd:double Annex XIII (1h)
Nominal voltage (dppk:voltageNominal) The suitable approximate value of the voltage used to designate or identify the battery. xsd:double Annex XIII (1h)
