Attributes

Information regarding the battery's power capability, power fade, and maximum permitted power.

governedBy: Annex XIII (1i); Art. 10

Properties

Property Description Type governedBy
Original power capability (dppk:powerOriginal) The amount of energy that a battery is capable to provide over a given period of time under reference conditions. xsd:double Annex XIII (1i); Art. 10; Annex IV Part B (4)
Remaining power capability (dppk:powerRemaining) Information on the remaining power capability as information on the state of health of the battery. xsd:double Art. 10; Annex IV (3); Annex VII Part A (2)
Power fade (dppk:powerFade) The decrease over time and upon usage in the amount of power that a battery can deliver at the rated voltage. xsd:double Annex IV Part A (4)
Maximum permitted battery power (dppk:powerMaximumPermitted) The value of maximum permitted power the battery is rated for. xsd:double Annex XIII (1i)
Ratio between nominal battery power and battery energy (dppk:powerEnergyRatio) Provides an indication of the applicable discharge and charge rate in terms of rated capacity (C-rate). xsd:double Annex IV Part B (2)
