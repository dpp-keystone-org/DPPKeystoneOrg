Attributes

Information regarding the battery's round trip energy efficiency and self-discharge rates.

governedBy: Annex XIII (1n); Art. 10

Properties

Property Description Type governedBy
Initial round trip energy efficiency (dppk:roundTripEfficiencyInitial) The ratio of the net energy delivered by a battery during a discharge test to the total energy required to restore the initial state of charge. xsd:double Annex XIII (1n); Art. 10; Annex IV (6)
Round trip energy efficiency at 50% of cycle life (dppk:roundTripEfficiencyAt50Cycles) Round trip energy efficiency measured at 50% of cycle-life. xsd:double Annex XIII 1(n); Annex IV (6)
Remaining round trip energy efficiency (dppk:roundTripEfficiencyRemaining) The in-use analogy to the initial roundtrip energy efficiency. xsd:double Art. 10; Annex VII Part A (3)
Energy round trip efficiency fade (dppk:roundTripEfficiencyFade) The decrease of round trip energy efficiency as percentage. xsd:double Annex IV Part A (4)
Initial self-discharge rate (dppk:selfDischargeInitial) The self-discharge-rate in an idle state of the battery in reference conditions at begin of life. xsd:double
Current self-discharge rate (dppk:selfDischargeCurrent) The change of the self-discharge rate in an idle state of the battery. xsd:double
Evolution of self-discharge rates (dppk:selfDischargeEvolution) The change of self-discharge over time and usage, calculated from the initial and current self-discharge rate. xsd:double Art. 14: Annex VII Part A (4)
