    Imports

    • https://dpp-keystone.org/spec/contexts/v1/dpp-core.context.jsonld

    Locally Defined Terms

    • Battery - A product that supplies electrical energy generated by direct conversion of chemical energy, as defined in Regulation (EU) 2023/1542.
    • BatteryCarbonFootprint - Information related to the carbon footprint of the battery, calculated according to the relevant Product Environmental Footprint Category Rules (PEFCR).
    • batteryCategory - The category of the battery (e.g., Portable, LMT, EV, Industrial, SLI).
    • batteryChemistry - The chemical composition of the battery (e.g., Lithium-ion, Nickel-metal hydride, Lead-acid).
    • ratedCapacity - The total electric charge the battery can deliver under specific conditions. Usually measured in Ampere-hours (Ah) or Watt-hours (Wh).
    • nominalVoltage
    • expectedLifetime - The anticipated service life of the battery, often expressed in charge/discharge cycles or years.
    • stateOfHealth - A measure of the battery's condition relative to its ideal new condition. Applicable to EV, LMT, and Industrial batteries.
    • batteryCarbonFootprint - Relates the battery to its declared carbon footprint information.
    • bcfTotal - The total declared carbon footprint of the battery, expressed in kg CO2 equivalent per functional unit.
    • bcfPerformanceClass
    • bcfDeclarationWebLink - A web link to a public version of the study supporting the carbon footprint declaration.

    Imports

    • https://dpp-keystone.org/spec/contexts/v1/dpp-core.context.jsonld

    Locally Defined Terms

    • TextileProduct - Any raw, semi-worked, or worked product which is exclusively composed of textile fibres, regardless of the mixing or assembly process employed.
    • FibreComposition - Details the specific type and percentage of textile fibres making up the product.
    • fibreComposition - Relates the textile product to its constituent fibres and their proportions.
    • fibreType - The specific type of fibre (e.g., Cotton, Polyester, Wool, Elastane).
    • fibrePercentage - The percentage of the total product weight constituted by this fibre.
    • animalOriginNonTextile - Indicates the presence of materials such as leather, bone, or shell.
    • tearStrength
    • abrasionResistance - Resistance to wear, often measured in Martindale cycles.
    • microplasticReleasePotential - Information regarding the propensity of the textile to release microplastics during washing or use.
    • careInstructions - Standardized care symbols (e.g., GINETEX codes).
    • repairabilityInformation - Instructions or scores related to the ease of repairing the textile product.
