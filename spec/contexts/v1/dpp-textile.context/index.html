Locally Defined Terms

  • TextileProduct - Any raw, semi-worked, or worked product which is exclusively composed of textile fibres, regardless of the mixing or assembly process employed.
  • FibreComposition - Details the specific type and percentage of textile fibres making up the product.
  • fibreComposition - Relates the textile product to its constituent fibres and their proportions.
  • fibreType - The specific type of fibre (e.g., Cotton, Polyester, Wool, Elastane).
  • fibrePercentage - The percentage of the total product weight constituted by this fibre.
  • animalOriginNonTextile - Indicates the presence of materials such as leather, bone, or shell.
  • tearStrength
  • abrasionResistance - Resistance to wear, often measured in Martindale cycles.
  • microplasticReleasePotential - Information regarding the propensity of the textile to release microplastics during washing or use.
  • careInstructions - Standardized care symbols (e.g., GINETEX codes).
  • repairabilityInformation - Instructions or scores related to the ease of repairing the textile product.
