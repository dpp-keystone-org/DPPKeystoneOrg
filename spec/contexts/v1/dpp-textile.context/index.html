Locally Defined Terms

  • TextileProduct - Any raw, semi-worked, or worked product which is exclusively composed of textile fibres, regardless of the mixing or assembly process employed.
  • FibreComposition - Details the specific type and percentage of textile fibres making up the product.
  • fibreComposition - Relates the textile product to its constituent fibres and their proportions.
  • fibreType - The specific type of fibre (e.g., Cotton, Polyester, Wool, Elastane).
  • fibrePercentage - The percentage of the total product weight constituted by this fibre.
  • productionSteps - The sequence of major production steps that occurred for this product.
  • step - The type of activity performed (e.g., Spinning, Weaving, Dyeing).
  • countryCode - The country code (ISO 3166-1 alpha-3) where the production step took place.
  • animalOriginNonTextile - Indicates the presence of materials such as leather, bone, or shell.
  • abrasionResistance - Resistance to wear, often measured in Martindale cycles.
  • cycles - Number of cycles (e.g., rubs) withstanded.
  • dimensionalStability - The percentage a fabric shrinks or grows after washing and drying.
  • warpChange - Dimensional change in the warp (length) direction.
  • weftChange - Dimensional change in the weft (width) direction.
  • colorfastnessToWashing - The resistance of the color to domestic laundering.
  • grade - The specific grade or value achieved (e.g. 1-5 scale).
  • microplasticRelease - Information regarding the amount of lint/particles released during washing.
  • value - The numeric value of the measurement.
  • apparelSize - The size of the textile product (e.g., 'M', 'L', '42').
  • apparelSizeSystem - The system used for the apparel size (e.g., 'EU', 'US', 'UK').
  • careInstructions - Standardized care symbols (e.g., GINETEX codes).
  • repairabilityInformation - Instructions or scores related to the ease of repairing the textile product.
