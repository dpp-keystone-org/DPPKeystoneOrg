Locally Defined Terms

  • cencpr
  • ConstructionProduct - Any product or kit which is produced and placed on the market for incorporation in a permanent manner in construction works or parts thereof, as defined by the Construction Products Regulation (CPR).
  • DeclarationOfPerformance - A document drawn up by the manufacturer expressing the performance of a construction product in relation to the essential characteristics of that product.
  • hasDoP - Relates the construction product to its Declaration of Performance.
  • dopIdentifier - The unique identification code of the Declaration of Performance.
  • declarationCode
  • declaredUses
  • latestDeinstallation
  • notifiedBody
  • technicalAssessmentBody
  • validationReports
  • europeanAssessmentDocument
  • harmonisedStandardReference - A reference (URI or identifier) to the applicable Harmonised European Standard (hEN) used for assessing the product.
  • notifiedBodyId - The identification number of the Notified Body involved in the assessment and verification of constancy of performance (e.g., NB1234).
  • avsSystem - The system for Assessment and Verification of Constancy of Performance (e.g., System 1, 1+, 2+, 3, 4).
  • reactionToFire - The classification of the product's contribution to fire (Euroclass).
  • thermalConductivity
  • compressiveStrength
  • steelGrade - The classification of the steel based on its composition and physical properties (e.g., R260, S355).
  • railProfile - The standardized cross-section design of a railway rail (e.g., 46E1).
