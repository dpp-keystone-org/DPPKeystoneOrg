Locally Defined Terms

  • dopc - Data block for Declaration of Performance and Conformity.
    • declarationCode - Unique identifier of the declaration.
    • dateOfIssue - The date the declaration was issued.
    • tensileStrengthAdhesion - Classification of adhesion.
    • modulusOfElasticityInCompression - Modulus of elasticity in compression.
      • method1
    • capillaryAbsorption - Capillary absorption rate.
    • dangerousSubstances - Statement on dangerous substances.
    • shrinkageAndExpansion
    • slipSkidResistance - Group of properties regarding slip/skid resistance.
      • laboratory - Measurements in the laboratory.
    • thermalExpansion - Change in volume in response to a change in temperature.
    • declaredUnit
    • functionalUnit
    • referenceServiceLife - The reference service life in years.
    • chlorideContent - Group of properties regarding chloride content.
      • 21DegC - Maximum percentage by mass.
    • bondStrength - Group of properties regarding bond strength.
      • 28Days - Bond strength measured after 28 days.
    • thermalCompatibility - Group of properties regarding thermal compatibility.
      • wetSurface - Thermal compatibility measured on wet surface.
    • resistanceToCarbonation - Group of properties regarding carbonation resistance.
      • 21DegC - Resistance to carbonation at 21 degrees Celsius.
    • reactionToFire - The classification of the product's contribution to fire (Euroclass).
      • avcpSystem1 - Reaction to fire under AVCP System 1.
      • avcpSystem2 - Reaction to fire under AVCP System 2.
      • avcpSystem3 - Reaction to fire under AVCP System 3.
      • avcpSystem4

      • methodA - Reference to Method A.
        • 25Percent - Elastic recovery using Method A at 25% elongation.
        • 60Percent - Elastic recovery using Method A at 60% elongation.
        • 100Percent - Elastic recovery using Method A at 25% elongation.
      • methodB - Reference to Method B.
        • 25Percent - Elastic recovery using Method B at 25% elongation.
        • 60Percent - Elastic recovery using Method B at 60% elongation.
        • 100Percent - Elastic recovery using Method B at 100% elongation.

      • procedureA - Reference to Procedure A.
