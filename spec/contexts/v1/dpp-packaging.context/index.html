Source: dpp-packaging.context.jsonld

Locally Defined Terms

  • packagingMaterials - A list of packaging elements associated with the product.
  • Packaging - Information about the materials and properties of the product's packaging.
  • packagingMaterialType - The type of material used for the packaging (e.g., 'Cardboard', 'Plastic').
  • packagingMaterialCompositionQuantity - The percentage of the material in the packaging.
  • packagingRecyclingProcessType - Indicates if the packaging is RECYCLABLE, REUSABLE, or COMPOSTABLE.
  • packagingRecycledContent - The percentage of recycled content in the packaging material.
  • packagingSubstanceOfConcern - Indicates if any substances of concern are present in the packaging.

Part of the DPP Keystone project.
