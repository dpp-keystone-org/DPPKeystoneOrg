Locally Defined Terms

  • DigitalProductPassport - A structured collection of product-related data defining the characteristics, provenance, and lifecycle information of a product, as mandated by the Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation (ESPR).
  • Organization - A general concept for any organization or legal entity involved in the product lifecycle.
  • PostalAddress - The physical mailing address of an organization or location.
  • Product - A general concept covering finished goods, components, or raw materials.
  • QuantitativeValue - A structured value representing a measurement, including the numeric value and the unit of measure.
  • DigitalDocument - A reference to an external digital file related to the product (e.g., manuals, reports, declarations).
  • RelatedResource - A resource identified by a URL, with additional metadata such as title, content type, and language.
  • Certification - A confirmation that the product meets the requirements of a specific standard or regulation (e.g., CE Marking, EPD).
  • SubstanceOfConcern - Details regarding dangerous substances or substances of very high concern (SVHC) present in the product.
  • EcoDesignProperty - A specific property related to the environmental performance or sustainability of the product, as required by Eco-Design regulations (e.g., Energy Efficiency, GWP).
  • ComplianceTopic - A declaration of compliance status regarding specific regulations (e.g., REACH, RoHS, Lacey Act).
  • Packaging - Information about the materials and properties of the product's packaging.
  • ProductionStep - A significant step in the manufacturing or processing chain of the product, including its location.
  • ProductCharacteristic - A specific, measurable or declared property of a product, such as its technical performance against a standard test.
  • EconomicOperatorRole - The role of an entity responsible for placing the product on the market, as defined by ESPR.
  • ManufacturerRole - The role of an entity responsible for the production of the finished goods.
  • FacilityRole - A specific location or site where manufacturing or processing activities occur.
  • ConformityAssessmentBodyRole - The role of an entity (e.g., Notified Body) that performs conformity assessment activities.
  • ISO6523 - An organization identifier structured according to ISO 6523-1.
  • digitalProductPassportId - The unique identifier for this specific Digital Product Passport instance. Should be a persistent, globally unique, and resolvable URI (e.g., URN UUID or HTTP URI).
  • uniqueProductIdentifier - The unique identifier for the physical product associated with this DPP. Aligns with the Unique Product Identifier (UPI) requirement in the ESPR. Strongly recommended to use established standards like GS1 Digital Link URI.
  • economicOperatorId - The unique identifier for the Economic Operator responsible for placing the product on the market.
  • facilityId - Optional. The unique identifier for the manufacturing or processing facility associated with the product.
  • granularity - Indicates the level of detail the DPP applies to: 'model' (product type), 'batch' (production lot), or 'item' (individual serialized unit).
  • dppStatus - The current lifecycle status of the DPP instance (e.g., 'active', 'inactive').
  • lastUpdate - The date and time when the DPP instance was last modified. Must be in ISO 8601 format (xsd:dateTime).
  • dppSchemaVersion
  • versionNumber - The version number of the DPP instance.
  • versionDate - The date of the version of the DPP instance.
  • contentSpecificationIds
  • verifiableCredential
  • hsCode - A code from the Harmonized System used to classify traded products.
  • unionDatabaseRegistrations - Registration numbers or identifiers from Union-level databases (e.g., EPREL).
  • economicOperator - Relates the DPP to the Economic Operator responsible for it.
  • manufacturer - Relates the DPP to the Manufacturer of the product.
  • manufacturingFacility - Relates the DPP to the specific facility where the product (or a step) was manufactured.
  • organizationName - The official legal name of the organization.
  • tradingName - The name under which the organization conducts business, if different from the legal name.
  • gln - A GS1 standard identifier for legal entities, functional departments, or physical locations.
  • leiCode - A standardized global identifier for legal entities.
  • organizationRole
  • additionalOrganizationID - An identifier for the organization other than GLN or LEI (e.g., EORI, VAT ID). Must be used in conjunction with additionalOrganizationIDType.
  • additionalOrganizationIDType - The type of the identifier provided in additionalOrganizationID (e.g., 'EORI', 'VAT').
  • iso6523 - Relates an organization to one of its ISO 6523 identifiers.
  • icd - The 4-digit International Code Designator (ICD) from ISO 6523-1.
  • iso6523Value - The value of the identifier within the scheme defined by the ICD.
  • address - Relates an organization to its physical address.
  • streetAddress - Includes street name and number.
  • postalCode
  • addressLocality
  • addressCountry
  • email
  • telephone
  • website - The URL of the organization's website.
  • productName - The common name of the product.
  • productCategory - A category for the product.
  • description - A detailed description of the product.
  • gtin - The GS1 identifier for the trade item.
  • brand
  • model
  • image - A URI pointing to an image of the product.
  • color
  • sizeCode
  • sizeSystemCode - The system used for the size code (e.g., EUROPE, US).
  • hasPart - Relates the main product to its constituent components or materials. This forms the Bill of Materials (BOM).
  • dppLink - A URI pointing to the Digital Product Passport of a component, if one exists.
  • materialClassification - A code or standard classifying the material (e.g., steel grade, plastic type).
  • countryOfOrigin - The country where the product or material was manufactured or produced. Recommended to use ISO 3166-1 alpha-3 codes.
  • weightPercentage - The percentage of the total weight of the main product that this component or material constitutes.
  • recycledContentPercentage - The percentage of the material that is derived from recycled sources.
  • productCharacteristics - Relates a product to its specific characteristics or performance properties.
  • characteristicName - The name of the characteristic being described (e.g., 'Bond strength', 'Reaction to fire').
  • characteristicValue - The measured or declared value of the characteristic (e.g., 'â‰¥ 2.0 MPa', 'Class A1').
  • testMethod - A reference to the standard or methodology used to determine the characteristic's value.
  • KgWeightLiteral - A literal value representing a weight in kilograms. A reasoner can expand this into a full dppk:QuantitativeValue.
  • MetersLengthLiteral - A literal value representing a length in meters. A reasoner can expand this into a full dppk:QuantitativeValue.
  • netWeight - The weight of the product or material, excluding packaging. Recommended to use specific datatypes like dppk:KgWeightLiteral.
  • length - The length of the product or material. Recommended to use specific datatypes like dppk:MetersLengthLiteral.
  • grossWeight - The weight of the product or material, including packaging, expressed as a QuantitativeValue.
  • value - The numeric value of the measurement.
  • unitCode - The unit of measurement, preferably using UN/CEFACT codes (e.g., KGM, MTR, LTR).
  • unitText - A human-readable representation of the unit (e.g., kg CO2e, Nm).
  • documents - Relates the product to associated digital documents.
  • certifications - Relates the product to its certifications.
