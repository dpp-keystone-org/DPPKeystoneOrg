Locally Defined Terms

  • Battery - A product that supplies electrical energy generated by direct conversion of chemical energy, as defined in Regulation (EU) 2023/1542.
  • BatteryCarbonFootprint - Information related to the carbon footprint of the battery, calculated according to the relevant Product Environmental Footprint Category Rules (PEFCR).
  • batteryCategory - The category of the battery (e.g., Portable, LMT, EV, Industrial, SLI).
  • batteryChemistry - The chemical composition of the battery (e.g., Lithium-ion, Nickel-metal hydride, Lead-acid).
  • ratedCapacity - The total electric charge the battery can deliver under specific conditions. Value provided in Ampere-hours (Ah).
  • nominalVoltage - The nominal voltage of the battery in Volts (V).
  • expectedLifetime - The anticipated service life of the battery, often expressed in charge/discharge cycles or years.
  • stateOfHealth - A measure of the battery's condition relative to its ideal new condition. Applicable to EV, LMT, and Industrial batteries.
  • batteryCarbonFootprint - Relates the battery to its declared carbon footprint information.
  • bcfTotal - The total declared carbon footprint of the battery per functional unit.
  • bcfPerformanceClass - The performance class of the battery's carbon footprint.
  • bcfDeclarationWebLink - A web link to a public version of the study supporting the carbon footprint declaration.
