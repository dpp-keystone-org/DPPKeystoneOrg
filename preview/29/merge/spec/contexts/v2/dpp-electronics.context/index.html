Locally Defined Terms

  • ElectronicDevice - Equipment which is dependent on electric currents or electromagnetic fields in order to work properly.
  • PowerTool - A tool that is actuated by an additional power source and mechanism other than the solely manual labor used with hand tools.
  • energyEfficiencyClass - The energy efficiency rating as defined by the EU Energy Labelling framework (e.g., A, B, C).
  • ipRating - Defines levels of sealing effectiveness against intrusion from foreign bodies (tools, dirt) and moisture (e.g., IP54).
  • sparePartsAvailable - Indicates whether spare parts are available for the product.
  • serviceManualAvailable - Indicates whether a service or repair manual is available.
  • softwareUpdatePolicy - Information on the duration and type of software/firmware updates provided by the manufacturer.
  • weeeCategory - The category under the Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment (WEEE) Directive.
  • disassemblyInstructions - Instructions for safely disassembling the product for repair or recycling.
  • torque - The rotational force generated by the tool, typically measured in Newton-meters (Nm).
  • maximumRatedSpeed - The maximum operating speed of the tool, typically measured in Revolutions Per Minute (RPM).
  • ratedPower - The power consumption of the device under normal operating conditions, typically measured in Watts (W).
  • voltage - The electric potential difference, typically measured in Volts (V).
  • maximumDrillingDiameter
  • chargingTime
  • warranty
