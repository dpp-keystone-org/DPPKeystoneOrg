Locally Defined Terms

  • IronSteelProduct - Data model for the Iron and Steel Passport according to EU ESPR.
  • heatNumber - The heat number for iron and steel to serve as a lot-level identifier.
  • productNumber - An internal identifier at the article level.
  • castNumber - Optional batch-level identifier representing the specific cast.
  • lotNumber - Optional batch-level identifier representing the specific manufacturing lot.
  • manufacturingDate - The date on which the product was manufactured.
  • purchaserOrder - Associated purchase order identifier.
  • steelGradeClassification - The classification of the steel grade.
  • steelDesignation - The specific designation or name of the steel.
  • technologyRoute - The primary manufacturing route (e.g. EAF, BF-BOF).
  • meltAndPourCountry - The country where the steel was originally melted and poured.
  • countryOfOrigin - The country where the product or material was manufactured or produced. Recommended to use ISO 3166-1 alpha-3 codes.
  • safeUseInstructions - Instructions and warnings for the safe use of the product.
  • endOfLifeInstructions - Instructions for dismantling, recycling, or disposing of the product.
  • recycledContentPercentage - Total recycled content percentage in the steel.
  • preConsumerRecycledContent - Percentage of pre-consumer recycled material.
  • postConsumerRecycledContent - Percentage of post-consumer recycled material.
  • recycledContentConformity - Reference or URI to the conformity declaration of the recycled content calculation.
  • cbamReportId - Identifier for the Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM) report.
  • reachDocumentation - URI link to the REACH regulatory documentation if applicable.
  • substancesOfConcern - Relates the product to any substances of concern it contains.
    • casNumber - Chemical Abstracts Service registry number.
    • ecNumber - European Community number for chemicals.
    • iupacName - International Union of Pure and Applied Chemistry nomenclature.
    • locationInProduct - Specific physical location of the component or substance inside the parent assembly.
