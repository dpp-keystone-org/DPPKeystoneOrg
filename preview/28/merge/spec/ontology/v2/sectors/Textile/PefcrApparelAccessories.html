Description

Garments for the head, neck, waist, or hands to protect from elements or secure clothing. Typical products included: Hats (Caps, flat caps, woollen hats/beanies, fedoras, panamas, bowlers, newsboys, berets), Scarves and ties (Warm and light scarves, buffs, neckerchiefs, headscarves, shawls, bowties), Belts (Dress belts, casual belts, buckle belts, tie-up belts, suspenders), Gloves and mittens (fingerless gloves, fashion gloves, outdoor sports gloves, mittens).
