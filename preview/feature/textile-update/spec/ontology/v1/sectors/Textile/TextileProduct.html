Attributes

Any raw, semi-worked, or worked product which is exclusively composed of textile fibres, regardless of the mixing or assembly process employed.

subClassOf: dppk:Product, schema:Product, gs1:Clothing

Properties

Property Description Type governedByequivalentPropertysubPropertyOf
Fibre Composition (dppk:fibreComposition) Relates the textile product to its constituent fibres and their proportions. dppk:FibreComposition Regulation (EU) No 1007/2011
Production Steps (dppk:productionSteps) The sequence of major production steps that occurred for this product. dppk:ProductionStep
Presence of Non-Textile Parts of Animal Origin (dppk:animalOriginNonTextile) Indicates the presence of materials such as leather, bone, or shell. xsd:boolean
Tear Strength (dppk:tearStrength) The force required to propagate a tear in the fabric. dppk:QuantitativeValue ISO 13937-2
Abrasion Resistance (dppk:abrasionResistance) Resistance to wear, often measured in Martindale cycles. dppk:QuantitativeValue ISO 12947
Dimensional Stability (dppk:dimensionalStability) The percentage a fabric shrinks or grows after washing and drying. dppk:QuantitativeValue ISO 5077
Colorfastness to Washing (dppk:colorfastnessToWashing) The resistance of the color to domestic laundering. dppk:QuantitativeValue ISO 105-C06
Microplastic Release (dppk:microplasticRelease) Information regarding the amount of lint/particles released during washing. dppk:QuantitativeValue ISO 4484-1
Care Instructions (dppk:careInstructions) Standardized care symbols (e.g., GINETEX codes). xsd:string ISO 3758
Repairability Information (dppk:repairabilityInformation) Instructions or scores related to the ease of repairing the textile product. rdf:langString
Apparel Size (dppk:apparelSize) The size of the textile product (e.g., 'M', 'L', '42'). xsd:string gs1:sizeCode
Apparel Size System (dppk:apparelSizeSystem) The system used for the apparel size (e.g., 'EU', 'US', 'UK'). xsd:string gs1:sizeSystemCode
PEFCR Category (dppk:pefcrCategory) The Product Environmental Footprint Category Rules (PEFCR) category to which the product belongs. dppk:PefcrCategoryValue Apparel and Footwear PEFCR v3.1
Textile ESPR Conformity Certifications (dppk:textileCertifications) Certifications specifically verifying ESPR textile claims (e.g. recycled content, recyclability, mechanical properties). dppk:Certification schema:hasCertification
EU Declaration of Conformity (dppk:euDeclarationOfConformity) A reference to the EU Declaration of Conformity for the product. dppk:RelatedResource
Carbon Footprint (dppk:carbonFootprint) The carbon footprint of the product. xsd:double
Carbon Footprint Benchmark Percentage (dppk:carbonFootprintBenchmarkPercentage) The product's carbon footprint performance compared to the established benchmark, expressed as a percentage. xsd:double
Robustness Score (dppk:robustnessScore) A numeric score representing the product's robustness (typically 0-10 scale). xsd:double
Recyclability Score (dppk:recyclabilityScore) A numeric score representing the product's recyclability (typically 0-10 scale). xsd:double
Spirality (dppk:spirality) The measured spirality of the textile product. xsd:double
Dimensional Change (dppk:dimensionalChange) The dimensional change (shrinkage or expansion) after testing. xsd:double
Visual Inspection Result (dppk:visualInspection) The result of the visual inspection (e.g. Pass/Fail or notes). xsd:string
Organic Content Percentage (dppk:organicContentPercentage) The percentage of organic material in the product. xsd:double
Organic Content Mass (dppk:organicContentMass) The absolute mass of organic material in the product. xsd:double
Pre-consumer Recycled Content (dppk:preConsumerRecycledContentPercentage) The percentage of pre-consumer recycled material in the product. xsd:double
Post-consumer Recycled Content (dppk:postConsumerRecycledContentPercentage) The percentage of post-consumer recycled material in the product. xsd:double
