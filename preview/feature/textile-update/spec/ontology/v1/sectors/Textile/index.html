Ontology: sectors

DPP Sector Ontology - Textile Definitions (v1)

Defines sector-specific concepts required for the Textile DPP, based on the EU Strategy for Sustainable and Circular Textiles. Acts as a bridge (Keystone) between Schema.org, GS1, UNECE, and EUDPP.
