Locally Defined Terms

  • mtc - A link to the Material Test Certificate associated with this product.
    • nominalSize - Nominal dimensional size of the steel product.
    • weightTolerance - Tolerated deviation in weight as per EN 10168.
    • yieldStrength - Yield strength parameter from the Mill Test Certificate.
    • yieldStrengthRatio - Ratio of yield strength to tensile strength (Re/Rm).
    • elongation - Percentage elongation at fracture.
    • relativeRibArea - Relative rib area on reinforcing steel products.
    • carbonContent - Carbon (C) content percentage defined in the MTC.
    • phosphorusContent - Phosphorus (P) content percentage.
    • sulfurContent - Sulfur (S) content percentage.
    • copperContent - Copper (Cu) content percentage.
    • nitrogenContent - Nitrogen (N) content percentage.
    • carbonEquivalent - Carbon equivalent value (CEV) for assessing weldability.
    • steelProcess - Details of the steelmaking or casting process.
    • finishing - Information regarding the surface finish or condition.
    • radiometricControl - Values associated with radiometric testing of the steel.
