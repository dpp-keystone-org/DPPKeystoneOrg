Description

Any product or kit which is produced and placed on the market for incorporation in a permanent manner in construction works or parts thereof, as defined by the Construction Products Regulation (CPR).

subClassOf: dppk:Product

Properties

Property Description Type
Has Declaration of Performance (dppk:hasDoP) Relates the construction product to its Declaration of Performance. dppk:DeclarationOfPerformance
Harmonised European Standard (hEN) (dppk:harmonisedStandardReference) A reference (URI or identifier) to the applicable Harmonised European Standard (hEN) used for assessing the product. xsd:anyURI
AVCP System (dppk:avsSystem) The system for Assessment and Verification of Constancy of Performance (e.g., System 1, 1+, 2+, 3, 4). xsd:string
Notified Body (NB) Details (dppk:notifiedBody) The EU-recognized notified body that verifies a product's conformance to European regulations dppk:Organization
Technical Assessment Body (TAB) Details (dppk:technicalAssessmentBody) The technical assessment body that independently evaluates a product's technical characteristics dppk:Organization
Validation Reports Reference (dppk:validationReports) Reference to reports validating the performance or conformity of the product. dppk:RelatedResource
European Assessment Document (EAD) Reference (dppk:europeanAssessmentDocument) A reference to the European Assessment Document (EAD) relevant to the product. dppk:RelatedResource
Reaction to Fire (dppk:reactionToFire) The classification of the product's contribution to fire (Euroclass). xsd:string
Thermal Conductivity (Lambda) (dppk:thermalConductivity) The property of a material to conduct heat. dppk:QuantitativeValue
Compressive Strength (dppk:compressiveStrength) The capacity of a material or structure to withstand loads tending to reduce size. dppk:QuantitativeValue
