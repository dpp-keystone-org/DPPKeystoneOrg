Description

Data model for the Battery Passport according to EU Battery Regulation.

subClassOf: dppk:Product

Properties

Property Description Type governedBy
Manufacturing place (dppk:manufacturingPlace) A battery passport must include the manufacturing place (geographical location of a battery manufacturing plant). The manufacturing place must also be provided on the battery label. xsd:string DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Manufacturing date (dppk:manufacturingDate) A battery passport must include the date of manufacture (month and year). The manufacturing date must also be provided on the battery label. Format YYYY-MM-DD (ISO 8601). xsd:date DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Date of putting the battery into service (dppk:serviceDate) Where appropriate, the battery passport must include information on the date of putting the battery into service. Format YYYY-MM-DD (ISO 8601). xsd:date DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Warranty period of the battery (dppk:warrantyPeriod) The battery passport must include information about the period for which the commercial warranty applies. xsd:string DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Battery category (dppk:batteryCategory) A battery passport must include the battery category. The battery category must be provided on the battery label. Categories: LMT battery, electric vehicle battery, or industrial battery. xsd:string DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Battery mass (dppk:batteryMass) A battery passport must include the battery mass. The battery mass must also be provided on the battery label. xsd:double Annex XIII (1a); Annex VI Part A (5)
Battery status (dppk:batteryStatus) A battery passport must include information on the status of the battery. Statuses: original, repurposed, re-used, remanufactured, or waste. xsd:string DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Separate collection symbol (dppk:separateCollectionSymbol) The battery passport must include the symbol for separate collection. The symbol should be translated into text to ensure machine readability. dppk:RelatedResource DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Symbols for cadmium and lead (dppk:heavyMetalSymbols) Batteries containing more than 0.002% cadmium or 0.004% lead must be marked with the chemical symbol (Cd or Pb). dppk:RelatedResource DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Carbon footprint label (dppk:carbonFootprintLabel) EV and rechargeable industrial batteries > 2 kWh and LMT batteries must bear a label indicating the carbon footprint and performance class. dppk:RelatedResource DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Extinguishing agent (dppk:extinguishingAgent) A battery passport must include the usable extinguishing agent. The information should be related to their possible area of application as regulated in DIN EN 2. xsd:string DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Meaning of labels and symbols (dppk:labelMeaning) Information regarding the prevention and management of waste batteries including the meaning of the labels and symbols. xsd:string DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Results of test reports proving compliance (dppk:testReports) A battery passport must include the test report results that can prove the compliance with the requirements stated in the battery regulation. dppk:RelatedResource DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Carbon footprint performance class (dppk:carbonFootprintClass) The battery passport must include the carbon footprint performance class in accordance with the delegated and implementing acts. xsd:string DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Web link to public carbon footprint study (dppk:carbonFootprintStudy) The battery passport must include a web link to the carbon footprint study accompanying the battery carbon footprint declaration. dppk:RelatedResource DIN DKE SPEC 99100
General battery and manufacturer information (dppk:carbonFootprintGeneralInfo) The battery passport must contain general battery and manufacturer information in relation to the battery carbon footprint. dppk:RelatedResource DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Absolute battery carbon footprint (dppk:carbonFootprintAbsolute) The battery passport should include the battery carbon footprint in absolute terms (kg CO2e). xsd:double
Information of due diligence report (dppk:dueDiligenceReport) The battery passport must include information on responsible sourcing as indicated in the report on its due diligence policy. dppk:RelatedResource DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Third party assurances of recognised schemes (dppk:supplyChainAssurances) The battery passport should contain information on third-party supply chain assurances such as certifications. dppk:RelatedResource DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Supply chain indices (dppk:supplyChainIndices) The battery passport should contain supply chain indices to allow easy assessment for consumers on level of responsible sourcing. dppk:RelatedResource
Battery chemistry (dppk:batteryChemistry) The battery passport must contain information on the battery chemistry (cathode, anode, electrolyte). xsd:string DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Critical raw materials (dppk:criticalRawMaterials) The battery passport must contain information on the critical raw materials present in the battery in a concentration of more than 0.1%. xsd:string DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Materials used in cathode, anode and electrolyte (dppk:materialComposition) The battery passport must contain information on the detailed composition of the battery, including materials used in the cathode, anode and electrolyte. Reuses dppk:Component. dppk:Component DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Hazardous substances (dppk:hazardousSubstances) The battery passport must contain information on hazardous substances other than mercury, cadmium, or lead. Reuses dppk:Component. dppk:Component DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Impact of substances on environment, human health, safety, persons (dppk:hazardousSubstancesImpact) The battery passport must contain information on the impact of substances, in particular hazardous substances, on the environment and human health. xsd:string DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Dismantling information (dppk:dismantlingInformation) A battery passport must include dismantling information, including exploded diagrams, disassembly sequences, and tools required. dppk:RelatedResource DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Part numbers for components (dppk:partNumbers) A battery passport must include part numbers for components, provided together with contact details of sources for spare parts. dppk:RelatedResource DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Information on sources of spare parts (dppk:sparePartsSources) A battery passport must include contact details of sources for spare parts. dppk:RelatedResource DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Safety measures (dppk:safetyMeasures) A battery passport must include safety measures, provided via the instruction manual. dppk:RelatedResource DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Information on waste prevention and management (dppk:wastePreventionInfo) Information on the role of end-users in waste prevention, separate collection, and information on battery collection and treatment. dppk:RelatedResource DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Pre-consumer recycled material shares (dppk:preConsumerRecycledMaterialComposition) The percentage shares of Nickel, Cobalt, Lithium, and Lead recovered from battery manufacturing waste (pre-consumer). dppk:Component DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Post-consumer recycled material shares (dppk:postConsumerRecycledMaterialComposition) The percentage shares of Nickel, Cobalt, Lithium, and Lead recovered from waste (post-consumer). dppk:Component DIN DKE SPEC 99100
Renewable content share (dppk:renewableContent) A battery passport must include information about the share of renewable content. xsd:double Annex XIII (1f)
Performance and durability parameters (dppk:performance) A battery passport must include information on the performance and durability of the battery. dppk:BatteryPerformance DIN DKE SPEC 99100
