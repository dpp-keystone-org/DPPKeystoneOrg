Locally Defined Terms

  • pefcrCategory - The Product Environmental Footprint Category Rules (PEFCR) category to which the product belongs.
  • T-shirts - Garment to cover the upper body to the elbow. Typical products included: Singlets, t-shirts, polo shirts, other short-sleeved shirts.
  • Shirts and blouses - Garment to cover the upper body including the entire arm. Typical products included: Long-sleeved shirts, blouses, tunics, base layers.
  • Sweaters and midlayers - Garment to keep the upper body warm and covered. Typical products included: Pullovers, cardigans, hoodies, jerseys, sweatshirts, knitted and wool sweaters, vests.
  • Jackets and coats - Garment to put on top of a shirt or sweater or to protect from the elements. Typical products included: Blazers, suit jackets, overcoats, other light jackets, rain jackets, outdoor winter jackets, parkas, down jackets, fur jackets, outdoor vests, leather jackets.
  • Pants and shorts - Garment to cover the lower body, may protect from the elements. Typical products included: Casual pants, outdoor pants, dress pants, jeans, sports pants, capri pants, shorts.
  • Dresses, skirts and jumpsuits - One-piece garment that covers both the upper and lower body, or the lower body only, other than pants and shorts. Typical products included: Short- and long-sleeved, strapless, wrap, long and short, one-piece suits.
  • Leggings, stockings, tights and socks - Tight garment to cover the legs and/or feet. Typical products included: Opaque and sheer tights, pantyhose, fishnets, ankle socks, knee socks, low-cut socks.
  • Underwear - Garment worn under clothes, often next to the skin of the upper or lower body. Typical products included: Boxers, briefs, panties, bras, body-shaping suits.
  • Swimwear - Garment worn for water-based or sunbased activities. Typical products included: Bikinis, bathing suits, racing-style swimwear, board shorts.
  • Apparel Accessories - Garments for the head, neck, waist, or hands to protect from elements or secure clothing. Typical products included: Hats (Caps, flat caps, woollen hats/beanies, fedoras, panamas, bowlers, newsboys, berets), Scarves and ties (Warm and light scarves, buffs, neckerchiefs, headscarves, shawls, bowties), Belts (Dress belts, casual belts, buckle belts, tie-up belts, suspenders), Gloves and mittens (fingerless gloves, fashion gloves, outdoor sports gloves, mittens).
  • Open-toed shoes - Open-toed shoes, providing protection from the ground. Used in pairs. Typical products included: Flip-flops, casual /fashion sandals, sports sandals, slippers.
  • Closed-toed shoes - Closed-toed shoes, providing protection from the ground. They may protect against water, the elements and/or heavy objects. Used in pairs. Typical products included: Slippers, tennis shoes, moccasins, espadrilles, sneakers, cleats, athletic shoes, dress shoes, protective toecap – toed shoes.
  • Boots - Boots that cover the ankle, providing protection from the ground. They may protect against water, the elements, heavy objects and/or ankle injuries. Used in pairs. Typical products included: Polymer boots, winter boots, hiking boots, dress boots, protective toecap - toed boots.
  • manufacturer - Relates the DPP to the Manufacturer of the product.
    • email - The email address for contacting the economic operator.
    • telephone - The telephone number for contacting the economic operator.
  • importer - Relates the DPP to the Importer of the product.
    • email - The email address for contacting the economic operator.
    • telephone - The telephone number for contacting the economic operator.
  • economicOperator - Relates the DPP to the Economic Operator responsible for it.
    • email - The email address for contacting the economic operator.
    • telephone - The telephone number for contacting the economic operator.
  • substancesOfConcern - Substances of concern present in the textile product.
    • name - The specific name of the component.
    • percentage - The percentage contribution of this component to the parent assembly (e.g., by weight).
    • uniqueProductIdentifier - The unique product identifier of the component.
    • casNumber - The Chemical Abstracts Service (CAS) registry number for the component.
    • ecNumber - The European Community (EC) number for the component.
    • iupacName - The International Union of Pure and Applied Chemistry nomenclature for the component.
    • locationInProduct - Specific physical location of the component or substance inside the parent assembly.
  • fibreComposition - Relates the textile product to its constituent fibres and their proportions.
    • name - The specific name of the component.
    • percentage - The percentage contribution of this component to the parent assembly (e.g., by weight).
    • uniqueProductIdentifier - The unique product identifier of the component.
    • casNumber - The Chemical Abstracts Service (CAS) registry number for the component.
    • ecNumber - The European Community (EC) number for the component.
    • iupacName - The International Union of Pure and Applied Chemistry nomenclature for the component.
    • locationInProduct - Specific physical location of the component or substance inside the parent assembly.
  • weightExcludingTrims - The weight of the product excluding non-textile trims (e.g. buttons, zippers), used as a base for calculations.
  • organicContentPercentage - The percentage of organic material in the product.
  • organicContentMass - The absolute mass of organic material in the product.
  • preConsumerRecycledContentPercentage - The percentage of pre-consumer recycled material in the product.
  • preConsumerRecycledContentMass - The absolute mass of pre-consumer recycled material in the product.
  • preConsumerTypeOfWaste - The classification or source of the pre-consumer waste used for recycled content.
  • postConsumerRecycledContentPercentage - The percentage of post-consumer recycled material in the product.
  • postConsumerRecycledContentMass - The absolute mass of post-consumer recycled material in the product.
  • postConsumerTypeOfWaste - The classification or source of the post-consumer waste used for recycled content.
  • euDeclarationOfConformity - A reference to the EU Declaration of Conformity for the product.
  • textileCertifications - Certifications specifically verifying ESPR textile claims (e.g. recycled content, recyclability, mechanical properties).
    • name - The name of the certification.
    • identifier - The unique identifier or number of the certification.
    • url - A web link to the certification details or registry.
    • certificationBodyName - The name of the body that issued the certification.
    • certificationBodyId - The unique identifier of the certifying body.
    • certificationStartDate - The date when the certification became valid.
    • certificationEndDate - The date when the certification expires.
    • certificationBodyRole - The role or capacity in which the certifying body issued the certification.
    • document - Relates the product to associated digital documents.
  • euEcolabel - Indicates whether the product has been awarded the EU Ecolabel. The label should be found in the certifications array as well, if present.
  • careInstructions - Instructions and manuals for the care, washing, and maintenance of the textile product.
    • resourceTitle - The title of the related resource.
    • contentType - The media type (MIME type) of the resource (e.g., 'application/pdf', 'image/jpeg').
    • language - The language of the resource content (e.g., 'en', 'fr').
    • url - The location of the resource.
  • safeUseInstructions - Instructions and guidelines for the safe use and maintenance of the textile product.
    • resourceTitle - The title of the related resource.
    • contentType - The media type (MIME type) of the resource (e.g., 'application/pdf', 'image/jpeg').
    • language - The language of the resource content (e.g., 'en', 'fr').
    • url - The location of the resource.
  • endOfLifeInstructions - Instructions for the proper disassembly, recycling, or disposal of the textile product at the end of its life.
    • resourceTitle - The title of the related resource.
    • contentType - The media type (MIME type) of the resource (e.g., 'application/pdf', 'image/jpeg').
    • language - The language of the resource content (e.g., 'en', 'fr').
    • url - The location of the resource.
  • repairInstructions - Instructions on how to repair or replace components of the textile product.
    • resourceTitle - The title of the related resource.
    • contentType - The media type (MIME type) of the resource (e.g., 'application/pdf', 'image/jpeg').
    • language - The language of the resource content (e.g., 'en', 'fr').
    • url - The location of the resource.
  • warrantyDuration - The duration of the commercial warranty provided with the product.
  • repairServices
  • carbonFootprint - The carbon footprint of the product.
  • carbonFootprintBenchmarkPercentage - The product's carbon footprint performance compared to the established benchmark, expressed as a percentage.
  • carbonFootprintClass - The performance class of the product's carbon footprint.
  • environmentalFootprint - The absolute value of the product's environmental footprint.
  • environmentalFootprintBenchmarkPercentage - The product's environmental footprint performance compared to the established benchmark, expressed as a percentage.
  • environmentalFootprintClass - The performance class of the product's environmental footprint.
  • carbonFootprintCalculationParameters - A reference to the document or resource describing the parameters used for the carbon footprint calculation.
    • resourceTitle - The title of the related resource.
    • contentType - The media type (MIME type) of the resource (e.g., 'application/pdf', 'image/jpeg').
    • language - The language of the resource content (e.g., 'en', 'fr').
    • url - The location of the resource.
  • robustnessScore - A numeric score representing the product's robustness (typically 0-10 scale).
  • recyclabilityScore - A numeric score representing the product's recyclability (typically 0-10 scale).
  • spirality - The measured spirality of the textile product.
  • dimensionalChange - The dimensional change (shrinkage or expansion) after testing.
  • visualInspection - The result of the visual inspection (e.g. Pass/Fail or notes).
  • euApparelSize
    • sizeDesignation
    • primaryDimension
    • primaryDimensionValue
    • cupSize
    • secondaryDimensions
      • dimension
      • value
    • Chest girth
    • Neck girth
    • Waist girth
    • Hip girth
    • Height
    • Bust girth
    • Underbust girth
    • Foot length
    • Arm length
    • Inside leg length
  • productionSteps - The sequence of major production steps that occurred for this product.
    • step - The type of activity performed (e.g., Spinning, Weaving, Dyeing).
    • countryCode - The country code (ISO 3166-1 alpha-3) where the production step took place.
  • animalOriginNonTextile - Indicates whether the product contains non-textile parts of animal origin, as required by the EU Textile Labelling Regulation.
  • brand - The brand name of the product.
  • color - The color of the product.
