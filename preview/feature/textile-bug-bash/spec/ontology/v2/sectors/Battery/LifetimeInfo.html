Description

Information regarding the expected lifetime of the battery in years and cycles, including reference tests.

governedBy: Annex XIII (1j, 1k, 4a); Annex IV

Properties

Property Description Type governedBy
Expected lifetime in calendar years (dppk:expectedLifetimeYears) The expected life-time under the reference conditions for which they have been designed in calendar years. xsd:double Annex IV, Part A(5); Annex XIII (1); Annex XIII (4a)
Expected lifetime: Number of charge-discharge cycles (dppk:expectedLifetimeCycles) The expected life-time under the reference conditions for which they have been designed in terms of cycles. xsd:integer Annex IV A(5)
Number of full charging and discharging cycles (dppk:cycleCountFull) The value for the number of full charging and discharging cycles should result from the aggregation of partial cycles occurring in the use phase. xsd:integer Annex XIII (4d); Art. 14: Annex VII Part B (5)
Cycle-life reference test (dppk:referenceTestCycleLife) The reference test used for determining the expected battery lifetime in cycles. xsd:string Annex XIII (1j)
C-rate of relevant cycle-life test (dppk:cRateCycleTest) Applied charge and discharge rate in terms of rated capacity (C-rate) of relevant cycle-life reference test. xsd:double Annex XIII (1p)
Energy throughput (dppk:energyThroughput) The overall sum of the energy throughput over the battery lifetime at a specific time during usage. xsd:double Annex VII Part B (2)
Capacity throughput (dppk:capacityThroughput) The overall sum of the capacity throughput over the battery lifetime at a specific time during usage. xsd:double Annex VII Part B (3)
Capacity threshold for exhaustion (dppk:exhaustionCapacityThreshold) The percentage of SOCE, above which the battery is still considered operational. xsd:double Annex XIII (1k)
